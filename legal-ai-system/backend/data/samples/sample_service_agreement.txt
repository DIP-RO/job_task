WHEREAS, Provider agrees to provide such services under the terms and conditions contained herein;

Provider shall provide the following services to Client:
1.1 Cloud Infrastructure Management

2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for an initial term of twelve (12) months.
2.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice.
2.3 Termination for cause may occur immediately upon written notice if a material breach is not cured within fifteen (15) days.

3.1 Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $5,000 for the services described herein.
3.2 Payment is due within fifteen (15) days of invoice date.
3.3 Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

4. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

4.1 Provider shall maintain 99.5% uptime for Client's services.
4.2 If uptime falls below 99%, Client is entitled to a service credit equal to 10% of monthly fees for each 1% of downtime.

5.1 Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information.
5.2 Confidential information does not include information that is publicly available or independently developed.

6. LIABILITY LIMITATIONS

6.1 Except for breaches of confidentiality, neither party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.
6.2 Each party's liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the preceding twelve months.
